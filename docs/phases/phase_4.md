# Phase 4: ValueCase

## Goal
Compile a comprehensive business case from Phases 1-3 outputs.

## Prerequisites
- Phase 2 and Phase 3 gates passed

## Inputs
- ValueScope artifact (Phase 1)
- ZeroBase artifact (Phase 2)
- SmartStack artifact (Phase 3)
- Scenario assumptions

## Process
1. Scenario modeling (best/base/worst)
2. Investment sizing
3. Timeline projection
4. Executive summary compilation

## Gate Criteria
- Three scenarios modeled with traceable assumptions
- Financial figures verified
- Executive summary reviewed

## Outputs
- Scenario models
- Investment timeline
- Financial projections
- Executive summary

## Artifacts
- `valuecase_v1`: Business case
