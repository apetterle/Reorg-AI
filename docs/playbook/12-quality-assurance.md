# Chapter 12: Quality Assurance

## Objectives
- Ensure analysis quality through systematic checks
- Verify numeric accuracy
- Maintain evidence traceability

## Quality Checks
- **Data completeness**: Minimum fact/evidence counts per phase
- **Numeric verification**: All numbers traced to sources
- **Evidence coverage**: Every claim backed by evidence
- **PII compliance**: No unresolved PII in outputs
- **Gate reviews**: Automated + human checks before phase progression
