# Chapter 14: Risk Management

## Objectives
- Identify engagement risks
- Define mitigation strategies
- Monitor and manage risks throughout

## Risk Categories
- **Data risks**: Incomplete data, PII exposure, quality issues
- **Analysis risks**: Incorrect computations, fabricated numbers
- **Operational risks**: Timeline delays, resource constraints
- **Compliance risks**: LGPD violations, audit failures
- **Change risks**: Resistance to recommendations, adoption failure

See `docs/risk-register.md` for the platform risk register.
