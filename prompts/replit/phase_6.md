# Phase 6: AIPolicyCore — Implementation Prompt

## Objective
Establish AI governance framework and policies for the restructured organization, ensuring responsible AI adoption.

## Prerequisites
- Phases 1 through 5 completed

## Required Inputs
- Technology stack recommendations (SmartStack)
- Organizational design (OrgDNA)
- Regulatory requirements and compliance data

## Implementation Steps

1. **AI Use Case Inventory**: Catalog current and planned AI/ML use cases across the organization.

2. **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate AI risks per use case (bias, privacy, security, reliability).

3. **Policy Framework**: Draft governance policies covering data usage, model deployment, monitoring, and accountability.

4. **Compliance Mapping**: Map policies to regulatory requirements (LGPD, sector-specific regulations).

5. **Artifact Generation**: Store `aipolicycore_v1` artifact with policy framework and compliance matrix.

## Gate Criteria
- AI use case inventory complete
- Risk assessment for high-priority use cases
- Policy framework draft reviewed by legal/compliance
- LGPD compliance verified

## Outputs
- AI use case inventory
- Risk assessment matrix
- Governance policy framework
- Compliance mapping
- AIPolicyCore artifact
